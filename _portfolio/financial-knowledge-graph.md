**Context.** At KPMG LightHouse, I contributed to an implementation-focused financial regulatory knowledge graph for a banking client. The project transformed fragmented regulatory documents, internal rules, and compliance clauses into a structured knowledge base for compliance search, training, and rule relationship analysis.

**My contribution.** I supported ontology refinement, including entity categories, relationship definitions, label granularity, and compliance-oriented schema design. I assisted regulatory text preprocessing, labeling, and label-quality checks. In the later stage of the project, I experimented with Chinese LLM-assisted label augmentation for low-resource or ambiguous categories, followed by manual review to reduce synthetic-label noise.

I also supported Chinese financial-legal NLP workflows, including lexicon customization, entity recognition experiments, and domain-adapted BERT-style classification for document-level and passage-level regulatory text. I helped load structured entities and relationships into Neo4j and validate graph queries for compliance search and training scenarios.
